Griz in the NFL

Caleb Kidder

Caleb Kidder wore #37 his senior year at Montana. He was the only Grizzly player who had a legit chance at being drafted in this years class. He didn't get drafted, but he signed with the Minnesota Vikings after the draft and will get a tryout.

JR Nelson

JR missed half of the college season after failing a drug test, but he played well in the games he did appear in. The cornerback, #18, joined Trumaine Johnson as Grizzly defensive backs in the league after he signed with the Kansas City Chiefs.

Yamen Sanders

Yamen Sanders has the look of an NFL player, tall, athletic and incredibly strong. His ball skills are lacking, but his physical abilities earned him a try out for the Oakland Raiders.
