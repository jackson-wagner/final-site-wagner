Jackson Wagner

My name is Jackson Wagner. I am a junior at the University of Montana majoring in Journalism. I am from Anaconda, Montana, and my passions are sports, music, movies and writing. I plan on graduating in 2018 and want to get a job working for an online media company like The Ringer after I leave college.

Montana Kaimin

During my freshman year of college, I received an email from Jesse Flickenger, the Sports Editor of the Montana Kaimin. He hired me on as an intern, writing one sports story a week for the spring semester in 2015. The next year, I served the Kaimin as the Deputy Sports Editor, writing stories and also coming up with ideas for our other reporters. In the fall semester of 2016, I became the Sports Editor, writing a weekly column and editing all sports stories. After one semester working there, I became the Web Managing Editor, specializing in breaking news and our online presence. For my senior year, I will be moving back to the sports department to serve as the Sports Editor again.

High School Golf

Golf is a sport I have had a passion for my entire life. My parents and both of my siblings play, so I grew up with a golf club in my hand. After a youth career filled with some success, I went on to play four years of high school golf. During my senior season, I tied a school record during a regular season tournament with a 71 in Dillon. At the Class A State Tournament, I broke the school record in my second round, shooting a two-day total of 76-70-146 to win the State Championship.
